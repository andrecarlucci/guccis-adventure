## Technology

- Use Phasor v3.90.0
- Here is a good example of how to create a game: https://phaser.io/tutorials/making-your-first-phaser-3-game/part1
- Use strict typescript latest version

## Specification

- This is a 2D platform game where a cat needs to save all his unicorn friends.
- The game is super cute and colorful.
- The cat is white and has blue eyes with a touch of gray.

| id  | User Story                                                            | The Game                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| --- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | As a player, I want to control a cat character that can run and jump. | The player controls a cat that can move left, right, and jump using the arrow keys.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 2   | As a player, I want to grab cat treats.                               | The cat when touching a treat, glows, gain points and restores one health bar if not full.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| 3   | As a player, I want to jump really high                               | The player can press space to jump and space again while in the air to double jump.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| 4   | As a player, I want to fight regular foes                             | In each level many dogs are around. The player loses a health bar if it touches the dogs. The player can kill the dogs jumping on top of them. The player has 5 health bars. Dogs will follow the player when the player is on the grownd but will keep moving in the same direction as before when the player is in the air.                                                                         |
| 5   | As a player, I want to fight awesome bosses                           | At the end of each level, there is a boss. The boss moves always in the direction of the player, trying to hit him. The boos only starts to move and he sees the player. The player can jump and land on top of the boss to cause it damage. 6 damage points kills the boss. The boss drops a key. When the boss is visible on the screen, we can see his health bar on the right side of the screen. |
| 6   | As a player, I want to save the unicorns                              | At the end of each level, there is an unicorn locked in a jailbox. After fighting the boss, the player gets the key and can open the jail saving the unicorn.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 7   | As player, I want to have a superpower                                | For every dog killed, the super power bar grows by one. When it gets to 5, the power bar is full and it blinks. The bar cannot be bigger than 5. While the bar is full, the player is allowed to perform a super power. It rotates like a small tornado so its tail and claws kills any enemies close to it. The bar is then set to zero.                                                             |
| 8   | As a player, I want to play using my cellphone                        | The game has virtual controls with left, right, to the left side, and jump and superpower to the right side. These controls only appear when the game is loaded on a cellphone.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| 9   | As a player, I die if I don't have health bars anymore                | If the player has zero health bars at any point, a small animation accours and it dies. A message in the middle of the screen appears with "Game over". Pressing the jump button restarts the level.                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| 10  | As a player, I want interessant enemies                               | There are 2 types of dogs. They change in color and one is 2 times faster than the other. All the rest is the same.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
